Innovation

Innovation is at the core of every organization--non-profit, government, private or public, in the classroom, and even in our personal lives. Understanding how to innovate is necessary for us to achieve our goals. There is a reason why nine out of ten startups, and an even higher new product release percentenge, fail. It isn't complicated, but to understand how to create something new, first we need to understand why fundamental systems are in place. These systems drive our ability to succeed, or force failure. Startup Technologies are experts in gathering, reviewing, and detailing what systems are in place, how to implement important policies, and provide needed retrospectives to ensure continued success.

There is no such thing as "here's the solution", but understanding what forces are at work, and following simple pattern will guarantee success. The secret is committment to discovery, focusing on learning, and tehn gathering feedback to improve, continuously.
